Caulerpa
Dynamical States

How do plants keep track of time? The answer might lie in the out-of-equilibrium dynamical states of their cytoplasm. Using time-series analysis and unsupervised learning techniques, I try to get a closer look at the internal clock of Caulerpa - a gigantic unicellular alga.

Chaotic Flight
Simulation

How hard is it for flying insects to stablize their flights while staying agile? Simulations reveal that the simplest wing motions could result in spectacularly chaotic flight trajectories.
